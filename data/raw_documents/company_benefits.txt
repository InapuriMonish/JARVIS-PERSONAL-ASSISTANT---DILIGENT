SECTION 1: HEALTH AND WELLNESS BENEFITS
================================================================================

1.1 MEDICAL INSURANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We offer three medical plan options to meet your healthcare needs:

PLAN A: PPO PREMIUM
- Monthly Premium: $250 (employee only) / $600 (family)
- Deductible: $500 individual / $1,000 family
- Out-of-pocket max: $3,000 individual / $6,000 family
- Co-pay: $20 primary care / $40 specialist
- Prescription: $10 generic / $30 brand / $50 specialty
- Network: Nationwide PPO network, 500,000+ providers
- Best for: Employees who want flexibility and low co-pays

PLAN B: HMO VALUE
- Monthly Premium: $150 (employee only) / $400 (family)
- Deductible: $250 individual / $500 family
- Out-of-pocket max: $2,000 individual / $4,000 family
- Co-pay: $15 primary care / $30 specialist
- Prescription: $5 generic / $25 brand / $40 specialty
- Network: Regional HMO network (referrals required)
- Best for: Employees who prefer lower premiums and don't travel often

PLAN C: HIGH DEDUCTIBLE HEALTH PLAN (HDHP)
- Monthly Premium: $75 (employee only) / $200 (family)
- Deductible: $1,500 individual / $3,000 family
- Out-of-pocket max: $5,000 individual / $10,000 family
- Co-insurance: 80/20 after deductible
- HSA contribution: $500 company seed funding
- Best for: Healthy employees who want lowest premiums + tax savings

Note: Company pays 80% of employee premiums and 50% of dependent premiums.


1.2 DENTAL INSURANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DENTAL PLAN HIGHLIGHTS:
- Preventive care: 100% covered (cleanings, x-rays, exams)
- Basic care: 80% covered (fillings, extractions)
- Major care: 50% covered (crowns, bridges, dentures)
- Orthodontia: 50% covered, lifetime max $2,500
- Annual maximum: $2,000 per person
- Monthly premium: $25 (employee) / $65 (family)
- Waiting periods: None for preventive, 6 months for major


1.3 VISION INSURANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

VISION PLAN HIGHLIGHTS:
- Eye exam: $10 co-pay, once per year
- Frames allowance: $150 per year
- Contact lens allowance: $150 per year
- Lens coverage: 100% for standard single/bifocal/progressive
- Monthly premium: $10 (employee) / $25 (family)
- Network: VSP Choice network

SECTION 2: FINANCIAL BENEFITS
================================================================================

2.1 RETIREMENT SAVINGS - 401(K)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

BUILD YOUR FUTURE WITH OUR 401(K) PLAN:

Eligibility: After 30 days of employment
Contribution Limits: Up to IRS maximum ($23,000 for 2024)
Catch-up Contribution: Additional $7,500 if age 50+

COMPANY MATCH:
- 100% match on first 4% of salary contributed
- Example: $80,000 salary, contribute 4% ($3,200), company adds $3,200
- Maximum company match: $8,000/year

VESTING SCHEDULE:
- Employee contributions: 100% immediately vested
- Company match vesting:
  • Year 1: 25%
  • Year 2: 50%
  • Year 3: 75%
  • Year 4: 100%

INVESTMENT OPTIONS:
- 20+ fund options (target date, index, bond, international)
- Company stock option available (max 10% of balance)
- Free financial advisor consultations quarterly
- Auto-escalation option available


2.2 STOCK OPTIONS & EQUITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

EMPLOYEE STOCK PURCHASE PLAN (ESPP):
- Purchase company stock at 15% discount
- Contribute up to 15% of salary
- Purchase periods: June and December
- Immediate selling allowed (no holding requirement)

EQUITY GRANTS (for eligible positions):
- RSU grants for senior roles
- Stock options for leadership
- Annual refresh grants based on performance
- Vesting: 4-year schedule with 1-year cliff


2.3 BONUSES & INCENTIVES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ANNUAL PERFORMANCE BONUS:
- Target bonus: 10-15% of base salary
- Based on individual and company performance
- Paid in February for prior year
- Must be employed on payment date

PROJECT BONUSES:
- Spot bonuses for exceptional work
- $500 - $5,000 based on impact
- Manager nomination required

REFERRAL BONUS:
- $2,000 for successful employee referral
- $3,000 for hard-to-fill positions
- Paid after referee completes 90 days


2.4 OTHER FINANCIAL BENEFITS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

LIFE INSURANCE:
- Basic life: 2x annual salary (company paid)
- Supplemental life: Up to 5x salary (employee paid)
- Spouse coverage: Up to $100,000
- Child coverage: Up to $10,000

DISABILITY INSURANCE:
- Short-term disability: 60% of salary, up to 12 weeks
- Long-term disability: 60% of salary, after 12 weeks
- Both company-paid at no cost to employee

FLEXIBLE SPENDING ACCOUNTS (FSA):
- Healthcare FSA: Up to $3,050/year
- Dependent Care FSA: Up to $5,000/year
- Commuter FSA: Up to $300/month
- Carryover: $610 to next year

HEALTH SAVINGS ACCOUNT (HSA):
(For HDHP participants only)
- Individual: $4,150/year limit
- Family: $8,300/year limit
- Company seed: $500 annually
- Triple tax advantage
