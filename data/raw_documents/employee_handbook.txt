CHAPTER 1: WELCOME TO TECHCORP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Welcome to TechCorp! We're excited to have you join our team of innovators and 
problem-solvers. This handbook contains essential information about our company 
policies, benefits, and workplace expectations.

Our Mission: To empower businesses with cutting-edge technology solutions that 
drive growth and innovation.

Our Core Values:
• Innovation - We embrace new ideas and creative solutions
• Integrity - We act with honesty and transparency
• Collaboration - We work together to achieve common goals
• Excellence - We strive for the highest quality in everything we do
• Respect - We value diverse perspectives and treat everyone with dignity


CHAPTER 2: EMPLOYMENT BASICS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.1 Employment Types
- Full-Time: 40 hours per week, eligible for all benefits
- Part-Time: 20-30 hours per week, eligible for prorated benefits
- Contract: Project-based, benefits vary by contract terms
- Intern: Learning-focused positions, limited benefits

2.2 Work Hours
- Standard hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (with 1-hour lunch break)
- Core hours: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM (all employees must be available)
- Flexible scheduling available upon manager approval
- Overtime must be pre-approved by department manager

2.3 Remote Work Policy
- Hybrid model: 3 days in office, 2 days remote per week
- Core in-office days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Full remote work requires VP-level approval
- Home office equipment allowance: $500 one-time
- Internet stipend: $50/month for remote workers


CHAPTER 3: COMPENSATION & PAYROLL
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3.1 Pay Schedule
- Paid bi-weekly (every two weeks)
- Direct deposit required for all employees
- Pay stubs available through HR portal

3.2 Salary Reviews
- Annual salary reviews conducted in December
- Merit increases based on performance ratings
- Promotion opportunities reviewed in January and July

3.3 Bonus Programs
- Annual performance bonus: Up to 15% of base salary
- Project completion bonuses for exceptional work
- Referral bonus: $2,000 for successful employee referrals


CHAPTER 4: TIME OFF & LEAVE POLICIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4.1 Vacation Policy
- New employees (0-2 years): 15 days paid vacation annually
- After 3 years: 20 days paid vacation
- After 5 years: 25 days paid vacation
- After 10 years: 30 days paid vacation
- Vacation requests must be submitted 2 weeks in advance
- Maximum carryover: 5 unused days to next year
- Vacation blackout periods: First 2 weeks of January (fiscal year close)

4.2 Sick Leave
- 10 paid sick days per year
- Doctor's note required for absences of 3 or more consecutive days
- Mental health days are included in sick leave
- Unused sick days do not carry over

4.3 Personal Days
- 3 personal days per year
- Can be used for any personal matters
- 24-hour advance notice preferred

4.4 Holidays
TechCorp observes the following paid holidays:
• New Year's Day
• Martin Luther King Jr. Day
• Presidents' Day
• Memorial Day
• Independence Day
• Labor Day
• Thanksgiving Day
• Day after Thanksgiving
• Christmas Eve
• Christmas Day
• New Year's Eve (half day)

4.5 Parental Leave
- Primary caregiver: 16 weeks paid leave
- Secondary caregiver: 4 weeks paid leave
- Adoption: Same benefits as biological parents
- Flexible return-to-work options available
- Gradual return-to-work program: Start at 50% hours for first 2 weeks

4.6 Bereavement Leave
- Immediate family (spouse, parent, child, sibling): 5 days
- Extended family (grandparent, aunt, uncle): 3 days
- Close friend: 1 day


CHAPTER 5: WORKPLACE POLICIES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.1 Dress Code
- Monday through Thursday: Business casual
  • Collared shirts, blouses, dress pants, skirts
  • No jeans, sneakers, or athletic wear
- Friday: Casual dress permitted
  • Jeans allowed (no ripped or distressed)
  • Sneakers permitted
  • T-shirts with appropriate content
- Client meetings: Business formal required
  • Suits, ties, formal dresses
- Lab/warehouse areas: Safety equipment required
  • Closed-toe shoes mandatory
  • Safety glasses in designated areas
  • Hard hats in construction zones

5.2 Attendance Policy
- Employees are expected to be punctual
- Notify manager within 30 minutes of scheduled start time if late
- More than 3 unexcused absences in 90 days may result in disciplinary action
- Pattern of tardiness will be addressed during performance reviews

5.3 Code of Conduct
- Treat all colleagues with respect and professionalism
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed
- Confidential information must be protected
- Company resources are for business use only


CHAPTER 6: PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6.1 Performance Reviews
- Annual reviews conducted in December
- Mid-year check-ins in June (informal)
- Quarterly goal tracking with managers
- 360-degree feedback available for leadership roles

6.2 Rating Scale
- 5: Exceptional - Consistently exceeds expectations
- 4: Exceeds Expectations - Often performs above requirements
- 3: Meets Expectations - Solid, reliable performance
- 2: Needs Improvement - Some gaps to address
- 1: Unsatisfactory - Significant performance issues

6.3 Promotion Process
- Promotion cycles: January and July
- Must have minimum 1 year in current role
- Must have rating of 4 or above
- Manager recommendation required
- Interview with promotion committee


CHAPTER 7: TECHNOLOGY & SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7.1 Equipment
- Laptop provided on first day
- External monitor and peripherals available upon request
- Mobile phone stipend: $75/month for roles requiring constant availability
- Equipment must be returned upon termination

7.2 IT Security
- Strong passwords required (12+ characters, mixed case, numbers, symbols)
- Two-factor authentication mandatory for all systems
- No personal software installations without IT approval
- Report security incidents immediately to security@techcorp.com

7.3 Internet & Email Usage
- Limited personal use permitted during breaks
- No streaming services during work hours
- Professional tone required in all communications
- Company email should not be used for personal matters


CHAPTER 8: HEALTH & SAFETY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8.1 Safety Guidelines
- Report all injuries immediately, no matter how minor
- Emergency exits and fire extinguisher locations must be known
- First aid kits located in break rooms and reception areas
- Monthly safety drills conducted

8.2 Ergonomics
- Ergonomic assessments available upon request
- Standing desks provided based on medical recommendation
- Regular breaks encouraged (5 minutes every hour)
- Eye strain prevention: Follow 20-20-20 rule

8.3 Mental Health Support
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP) available 24/7
- 6 free counseling sessions per year
- Mental health days included in sick leave
- No stigma policy - support is confidential
